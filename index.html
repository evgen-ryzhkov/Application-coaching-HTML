Start building a career from the beginning is a challenging task. Starting a  career in a new country is twice harder.

There are so many questions. Where to start? Is my education worth something? Are my professional skills relevant here? What can I expect? Can I even apply for a good job?

And there are obstacles as well. Language barriers, bureaucracy, cultural differences.

Career Accelerator Program will answer all these questions and provide you with a clear guidance how to deal with the obstacles.

3 language to choose

The program can be provided in English, German or Russian of your choice.

Adapted to your schedule

Choose a start date and schedule coaching sessions on your preferred days and times.

Online or in classroom

Enjoy the convenience of online sessions. Or choose face-to-face meetings in our class in Cologne.
